Our Story

  • Community
  • History
2015-2016
Inception

Project founding team researches with several open source projects and pursues a market fit idea for a core banking platform. Both quantitative and qualitative research points to a open banking platform hypothesis.

Partnerships

Founding team generates product research traction and organizes hackathons, pursues case study partnerships with banks, NBFCs and fintech in South Asia.

2016-2018
2018-2020
Security

Smart Contracts accounting is introduced for enhanced security. Architecture and design principles are improved. Ease of development is investigated.

Case Studies

Core team continues to pursue case studies with banks, NBFCs and fintech in South Asia and North America. Team works on implementing compatible open source projects e.g Mifos X, Apache Fineract, Open Banking Gateway, Odoo with Finscale distribution.

2019-2020
2019-2021
Global

Project gets incubated by Muellners Foundation.

Adoption

Finscale is released as a developer version with a Developer console for enterprise implementations.

Future

Finscale® is maintained by Muellners Foundation.
