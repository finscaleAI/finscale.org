What is the fuss?

Start working with Finscale that can provide everything you need to easily build, deploy and scale financial applications.

Build, Launch and Run!

With its cloud native architecture, Finscale can be deployed anywhere with standalone applications.

Micro-services

Loosely based on micro-services design principles, you can generate financial web applications in a jiffy.

Inbuilt data security

Security is baked in Finscale with the use of sophisticated encryption schemes - the kind that power authentication mechanisms for Bitcoin, a peer to peer network.

Developer Friendly

Ease of coding in any language & framework of choice, we use open source solutions like Jhipster.

Highly Modular & Reliable

Finscale is a modern post Covid financial platform. Improves transaction reliability, brings modularity to a whole new level.

Scalability

Finscale employs and use a quasi-distributive architecture, to make sure that financial organisations scale with ease.

Retain your customers and scale your financial services business.

Finscale REST API endpoints, micro services design and easy to generate applications.

Stay on top of your Banking Business

You are a Fintech, neobank, challenger bank, Non banking financial company, a high street bank or a payments processor. Finscale helps you stay relevant in your sector.

  • Strengthen & Enhance your core banking capacities with a seamless integrations framework.
  • Consolidate your historical Financial Data by using a data streaming method.
  • Prepare your organisation for the Banking services of the future.
